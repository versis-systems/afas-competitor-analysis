### AFAS Software - Deep Dive

**Strengths:**
- Market leader with strong brand recognition
- Comprehensive Dutch payroll with 200+ CAO agreements
- All-in-one ERP platform (HR + Finance + Operations)
- Mobile app rated #1 business app in Netherlands
- Strong regulatory compliance (Belastingdienst, UWV)

**Weaknesses (Research-Based Pain Points):**

1. **Poor Financial Visibility** (Opportunity Score: 9/10)
   - Users cannot easily see remaining project budgets
   - Different answers from different people about budget status
   - Source: University of Groningen case study

2. **No Purchase Accountability** (Opportunity Score: 10/10)
   - Anyone can purchase using any project number
   - No way to identify who made anonymous purchases
   - Source: UKrant investigation

3. **Complex Time Calculations** (Opportunity Score: 7/10)
   - Must calculate exact minutes/seconds for FTE changes
   - Users describe as "ridiculous"

4. **Slow Implementation** (Opportunity Score: 8/10)
   - "Poorly supervised" with "too many time constraints"
   - Major flaws remained 2+ years post-launch

5. **Limited API/Integration** (Opportunity Score: 8/10)
   - Closed system with limited customization
   - Student administration disconnected

6. **Outdated UX/UI** (Opportunity Score: 7/10)
   - Described as "clunky, buggy, primitive"
   - Traditional design vs modern competitors

**Pricing:**
- Basic: €5-7/user/month (Core HR)
- Standard: €9-11/user/month (+ Recruitment, Time tracking)
- Premium: €13-16/user/month (+ Payroll, Performance)
- Enterprise: €59/month minimum (2 users included)
